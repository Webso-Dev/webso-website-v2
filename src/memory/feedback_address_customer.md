type: feedback
---

Do not use "yritys", "yrityksiä", or "yritysten" when addressing or referring to the customer/reader. Use second person instead: "sinulle" / "you". Form field labels ("Yritys" as a field name) are fine.

**Why:** Futurice does this — feels more direct and personal. Noted by Robert.

**How to apply:** Review any service/marketing copy that describes what we do "for companies" and replace with second-person framing. Form labels are exempt.
